thelec.net · 1h ago thelec.net · 1小时前

Micron has begun mass production of 1α-node (10nm-class fifth-gen) DDR4 at its expanded Manassas, Virginia fab — the first time that node has been produced in the United States. Yesterday's headline was that Manassas would 'quadruple US DDR4 output'; today's is that it's not just more DDR4 but the most-advanced DDR4 anyone is willing to make at scale, since the rest of the industry has shifted leading-edge capacity to DDR5 and HBM. The strategic value here is dual: a domestic-source for automotive and defense, and continued profitability on a node everyone else abandoned. 美光已在弗吉尼亚州马纳萨斯扩建厂开始1α节点(10nm级第五代)DDR4的量产——这是美国本土首次生产该节点产品。昨日的标题是'美国DDR4产能将翻两番';今日补足的是:不仅产能更高,而且是业内仍愿意大规模生产的最先进DDR4,因为其他厂商已将先进产能转向DDR5与HBM。战略价值有二:为汽车与国防提供本土来源,以及在他人放弃的节点上维持盈利能力。
