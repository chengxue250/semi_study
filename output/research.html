Electric-Field Mapping of Optically Perturbed CdTe Radiation Detectors 光扰动CdTe辐射探测器的电场映射

A. Cola, L. Dominici, A. Valletta

arXiv:2606.13622 · 2026-06-11T17:36:26Z

The paper maps the two-dimensional electric-field vector and its time evolution inside a Schottky CdTe detector after localized optical exposure, using the Pockels effect and custom image processing. The measured dynamics agree with simulations based on a dominant two-level deep-trap model, giving a compact explanation for space-charge-driven field distortion. This is a device-level diagnostic result rather than a manufacturing demonstration, but it could improve failure analysis and operating models for CdTe radiation sensors. 论文利用Pockels效应和定制图像处理，在局部光照后测量Schottky CdTe探测器内部二维电场矢量及其时间演化。实测动态与基于主导双能级深陷阱模型的仿真一致，为空间电荷引起的电场畸变提供了简洁解释。该工作属于器件级诊断，而非制造验证，但可改进CdTe辐射传感器的失效分析和工作模型。

A Micron-Scale Three-Port Magnonic Circulator 微米级三端口磁振子环行器

K. S. Weber, L. Temdie, V. Castel, et al.

arXiv:2606.13540 · 2026-06-11T16:24:59Z

The authors experimentally demonstrate spin-wave circulation across all three ports of a micron-scale rectilinear magnonic device. Chiral excitation from nanowire gratings produces directional channels, while the operating band can be tuned from 2 to 8GHz with fields up to 100mT and grating geometry. The result is a prototype with a narrow operating band, but it provides measured evidence for integrated non-reciprocal microwave components at dimensions far below conventional circulators. 作者在微米级直线型磁振子器件上，通过完整三端口谱学实验验证了端口之间的自旋波环行。纳米线光栅的手性激发形成定向通道，工作频段可借助最高100mT外磁场和光栅几何在2至8GHz范围内调节。该原型的工作带宽仍较窄，但为尺寸远小于传统环行器的集成非互易微波器件提供了实验证据。

ReSCom: A Reconfigurable Stochastic-Computing SNN Accelerator ReSCom：基于随机计算的可重构SNN加速器

A. A. Fereidani, M. R. Roshanshah, S. Safari

arXiv:2606.13560 · 2026-06-11T16:44:08Z

ReSCom uses stochastic multiplication for neuron dynamics while retaining exact fixed-point addition and subtraction, allowing runtime trade-offs among accuracy, latency, and energy. One unified datapath supports integrate-and-fire, leaky integrate-and-fire, and synaptic neuron models; an Artix-7 FPGA implementation reaches 92.80% MNIST accuracy. The result is useful architectural evidence, but it is an FPGA study on a small benchmark rather than measured ASIC silicon or a modern large-scale SNN workload. ReSCom在神经元动态中采用随机乘法，同时保留精确的定点加减法，从而可在运行时权衡精度、延迟与能耗。统一数据通路支持IF、LIF和Synaptic三类神经元模型，在Artix-7 FPGA上实现92.80%的MNIST准确率。该结果具有架构参考价值，但仍是小型基准上的FPGA研究，并非ASIC实测硅片或现代大规模SNN负载。

Optical Equilibrium Propagation on a Spatial Photonic Ising Machine 空间光子Ising机上的光学平衡传播

D. Vanden Abeele, D. Veraldi, D. Pierangeli, et al.

arXiv:2606.13454 · 2026-06-11T15:12:05Z

The work demonstrates a hybrid optical-digital implementation of equilibrium propagation using a spatial photonic Ising machine. A spatial light modulator encodes continuous neuron states and rank-1 binary trainable patterns as optical phase, with an experimental evaluation on the Wine dataset and numerical scaling work on MNIST. It is a concrete physical implementation, but the larger-task evidence remains simulated and the setup is not yet an integrated photonic accelerator. 该工作利用空间光子Ising机实现了混合光电平衡传播。空间光调制器把连续神经元状态和秩1二值可训练模式编码为光学相位，实验部分使用Wine数据集，向MNIST扩展的结果则来自数值研究。它给出了具体的物理实现，但更大任务的证据仍停留在仿真阶段，系统也尚未成为集成光子加速器。

Early Exit at the Commitment Boundary Cuts Reasoning Length 在承诺边界提前退出可缩短推理链

D. Scalena, S. Candussio, L. Bortolussi, et al.

arXiv:2606.13603 · 2026-06-11T17:21:16Z

The paper identifies a commitment boundary where a reasoning model's answer becomes stable, after which many chain-of-thought steps no longer change the answer probability. Attention-based probes predict this transition across unseen tasks, enabling early exit that reduces reasoning length by up to 55% on average with negligible performance loss. The systems payoff is direct in lower token generation and accelerator occupancy, though the approach still depends on probe reliability across model families and deployment distributions. 论文识别出一个「承诺边界」：推理模型的答案在此处趋于稳定，之后大量思维链步骤不再改变答案概率。基于注意力的探针可在未见任务上预测这一转折点，使平均推理长度最多降低55%，同时性能损失可忽略。其系统收益是直接减少token生成量和加速器占用，但实际部署仍取决于探针在不同模型家族和数据分布上的可靠性。

GF-DiT Dynamically Reschedules GPU Parallelism for Diffusion Serving GF-DiT为扩散模型服务动态重调度GPU并行度

X. Qiang, Y. Hu, S. Sun, et al.

arXiv:2606.13501 · 2026-06-11T15:51:10Z

GF-DiT treats GPU parallelism as a schedulable resource instead of fixing one configuration for an entire diffusion-transformer request. Its runtime splits requests into independently schedulable trajectory tasks and uses group-free collectives to re-form execution groups online; the implementation is built in vLLM-Omni. The architecture targets utilization and service-quality losses caused by heterogeneous requests, but the candidate abstract is truncated before quantitative evaluation results, so the magnitude of the gain cannot be assessed here. GF-DiT不再为整个Diffusion Transformer请求固定一种并行配置，而是把GPU并行度视为可调度资源。其运行时将请求拆分为可独立调度的轨迹任务，并通过无固定组collective在线重组执行组，系统实现基于vLLM-Omni。该架构针对异构请求造成的利用率和服务质量损失，但候选摘要在量化评测结果前被截断，因此目前无法判断收益幅度。

Orchestration Reward Modeling Reduces Multi-Agent Training Tokens 编排奖励模型降低多智能体训练token开销

K. Y. Tsang, Z. Zhao, V. Venkataramani, et al.

arXiv:2606.13598 · 2026-06-11T17:16:24Z

OrchRM trains an orchestration-level reward model from intermediate multi-agent artifacts, avoiding repeated costly sub-agent rollouts for supervision. The authors report up to a 10x reduction in training-token use and accuracy gains of up to 8% for multi-agent test-time scaling across reasoning and web tasks. The hardware relevance comes from reducing generated and processed tokens, but the claims are preprint results and do not include energy or accelerator-level measurements. OrchRM利用多智能体执行过程中的中间产物训练编排层奖励模型，避免为监督反复进行高成本子智能体rollout。作者报告训练token用量最多降低10倍，多智能体测试时扩展在推理和Web任务上的准确率最高提升8%。其硬件意义在于减少生成和处理的token，但这些仍是预印本结果，尚无能耗或加速器层面的测量。

A Single Program Specifies Both Hardware Drivers and Monitors 用同一程序同时定义硬件驱动器与监视器

E. Ng, N. Shyamsunder, F. Pham, et al.

arXiv:2606.13659 · 2026-06-11T17:56:13Z

The proposed DSL expresses a hardware communication protocol once and derives both stimulus-driving behavior and transaction monitoring from the same imperative program. Given a waveform, the associated tool infers a transaction-level trace consistent with that specification, which could eliminate mismatches between separately maintained drivers and monitors. The paper is still a design proposal with planned evaluation on Wishbone and AXI-Stream, so real-world coverage, ambiguity handling, and runtime cost remain open. 该DSL只需用一个命令式程序描述硬件通信协议，即可同时导出激励驱动行为和事务监视逻辑。配套工具能够根据波形推断与规范一致的事务级轨迹，有望消除驱动器与监视器分开维护造成的不一致。论文目前仍是设计提案，计划在Wishbone和AXI-Stream上评估，因此真实协议覆盖、歧义处理和运行成本仍待验证。
